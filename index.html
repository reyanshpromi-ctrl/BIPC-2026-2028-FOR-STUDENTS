SESSION 01: THE FOUNDATION

Units, Dimensions & Real-life Physics

Physics is the study of how the universe behaves. In your NEET preparation, Accuracy is your best friend. Every measurement requires a unit—imagine a doctor prescribing "5 medicine." 5 grams? 5 ml? 5 tablets? Units save lives!

Core Lesson

Dimensional analysis helps us verify if an equation is correct. For example, Force = Mass × Acceleration. $[M] \times [LT^{-2}] = [MLT^{-2}]$.

Real Life Application

Satellites use precise dimensional calculations to stay in orbit. A small error in a unit conversion once crashed a $125 million Mars orbiter!
